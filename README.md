## ðŸš€ Overview

**Job Search Match (Manager Mode)** is a **decision-oriented job discovery and filtering application** built specifically for **senior analytics professionals**.

This is **not** a mass job scraper or ATS keyword matcher.

The app is designed around how **real senior hiring decisions are made**:

- Role seniority matters more than tools  
- Decision ownership matters more than dashboards

The outcome is deliberate:

**Fewer roles. Higher relevance. Zero noise.**
